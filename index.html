This is a histogram of the homework grades of the Farmer Penguin, created using Google Sheets. I entered the grades in a column on the sheet, highlighted them, and created a histogram with the create chart option. I then adjusted the bucket size - the size of the groupings on the x-axis. I chose 5 because I thought it best showed in general the penguin's most common grades. It is clear from the histogram that the penguin commonly gets between a 34 and 39 on homework assignments.
